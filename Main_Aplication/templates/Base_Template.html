General Lower Limb Amputation Information

1. Postsurgical Stabilization

This step takes place at the acute hospital and involves surgery and stabilization thereafter, so that patients can be safely discharged to the next level of care.

2. Preprosthetic Rehabilitation

Preprosthetic Rehabilitation occurs at the Inpatient Rehabilitation Facility and aims for functional independence and medical stability at the wheelchair/walker level to ensure a safe discharge to the community.

3. Limb Healing & Maturation

Limb Healing & Maturation happens in the community setting. Together, steps 2 and 3 correspond to the preprosthetic phase

4. Prosthetic Fitting

Prosthetic Fitting occurs in the community setting and involves prosthetic clinic visits and time taken to make a well-fitting prosthesis.

5. Prosthetic Rehabilitation

This step takes place at the Inpatient Rehabilitation Facility and aims to achieve functional independence and medical stability at the prosthetic device level for safe discharge to the community.
